What is Candy Crush about?


Gameplay

The main objective is to match three or more candies of the same color by swapping adjacent candies on a game board. Matching candies creates special candies and triggers chain reactions, earning players points. Each level has specific objectives, such as reaching a certain score, clearing jelly, or bringing down ingredients.

Levels and Episodes

"Candy Crush" is divided into levels, grouped into episodes. New levels and episodes are periodically added through updates. As players progress, they encounter different challenges and obstacles, including blockers, bombs, and more complex level designs.

Who created Candy Crush?


"Candy Crush" was created by the game development studio King. The company, originally known as King.com, was founded in 2003 by Riccardo Zacconi, Sebastian Knutsson, Thomas Hartwig, Lars Markgren, and Patrik Stymne. The headquarters is located in London, United Kingdom..
